U15 and U18 Biathlon Camp

Join our action-packed Biathlon Camps this July! Perfect for U15 and U18 athletes eager to sharpen their skills in a vibrant environment. Experience the thrill of shooting on our 30-point range and glide through our scenic rollerski loop. No prior shooting experience needed for U15s, while U18s require rollerski experience. U15 Biathlon Camp July 22 -July 25:

Come join us for our U15 Biathlon Camp! Camp participants will get to practice their biathlon skills on our full 30 point shooting range under the guidance of our experienced coaches. Campers will also get lots of time on rollerskis in a safe, car-free environment on our 3km rollerski loop. This is a perfect opportunity for cross country skiers to get a strong introduction to biathlon, as well as for experienced biathletes to practice their skills. Training will mostly be rollerskiing, but campers should also bring running sneakers, and be prepared for some easy runs. Prior shooting experience is not needed. Prior rollerski experience recommended, but not required. We will provide a rifle for you if you need one.

Housing: Bunk beds and cots will be provided in the heated lodge and wax rooms
Food: We will provide meals and a few group snacks, but encourage campers to bring personal snacks. Please communicate any allergies/dietary restrictions to us.

Camp Fee: $350

U18 Biathlon Camp July 27 -July 31:

Come join us for our U18 Biathlon Camp! Camp participants will get to hone their biathlon skills on our full 30 point shooting range under the guidance of our experienced coaches. Campers will also get lots of time on rollerskis in a safe, car-free environment on our 3km rollerski loop. This is a perfect opportunity for cross country skiers to get a strong introduction to biathlon, as well as for experienced biathletes to practice their skills. Prior rollerski experience is required, however, prior shooting experience is not. Training will mostly be rollerskiing, but campers should also bring running sneakers, and are encouraged to bring mountain or gravel bikes to explore the trails during non-rollerski sessions. We will provide a rifle for you if you need one.

Housing: Bunk beds and cots will be provided in the heated lodge and wax rooms
Food: We will provide meals and a few group snacks, but encourage campers to bring personal snacks. Please communicate any allergies/dietary restrictions to us.
