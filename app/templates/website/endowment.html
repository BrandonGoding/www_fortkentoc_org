Fort Kent Outdoor Center Endowment

Securing the future of year-round outdoor recreation in the St. John Valley.

Background

Since the venue’s creation in 1998 as the Maine Winter Sports Center, the Libra Foundation of Portland, Maine was the primary funder of operations in Fort Kent and at the Nordic Heritage Center in Presque Isle.

In 2024, with the closure of the Nordic Heritage Center, all FKOC assets were gifted to our operating Board of Directors. For fifteen years, Libra support helped cover capital improvements, maintenance, taxes, insurance, and half of the venue manager’s salary. That support has now ended, making it increasingly difficult to maintain our daily operations and programming.

To meet this challenge, FKOC has created an endowment fund with the Maine Community Foundation to generate annual interest and replace the former contributions. Long-time supporter Phyllis Jalbert has endorsed the initiative and provided the largest contribution to date.
