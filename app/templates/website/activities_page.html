Year Round Outdoor Activities

Maine enjoys four full seasons, each beautiful and fun-filled in their own way. In the spring, summer, and fall, visitors and members of the Fort Kent Outdoor Center can enjoy mountain biking, disc golfing, and hiking through a deciduous forest. In the winter months, FKOC boasts some of the best-groomed trails in New England for nordic skiing and snowshoeing. Our sledding hill is always a draw for families looking to get outside as well.
