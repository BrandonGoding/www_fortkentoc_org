US Biathlon National Championships

This year’s US Biathlon National Championships and the inaugural U.S. Biathlon Collegiate National Championships will take place in Fort Kent, Maine March 21-24, 2024. The three-day race series will include a sprint race, which will double as the first-ever U.S. Biathlon Collegiate National Championships, a pursuit race, and relay races. As a past host of 2004 and 2011 Biathlon World Cup events, the trails in Fort Kent are truly world class and the town is blessed with some of the best natural snow in the U.S.
