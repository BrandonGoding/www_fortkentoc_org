Memberships

Our memberships follow the calendar year from January 1 to December 31.

Becoming a FKOC member

All members will receive a membership card in the mail, once the waiver form has been signed. Membership includes access to our beautiful trails year-round for Cross Country Skiing, Snowshoeing, Disc Golf, Mountain Biking, Roller-skiing, Running, and Walking. The Lodge will be open daily, once ski season has started and close again in late April. Please take care to keep the Lodge clean and orderly, as it is staffed by volunteers.

Membership is the lifeblood of the FKOC, providing the primary source of funding that sustains our operations and allows us to offer an array of programs and services. Your membership dollars go directly toward maintaining our venue, grooming ski trails, and ensuring the year- round excellence of our facilities. By becoming a member, you not only gain access to incredible outdoor experiences but also play a pivotal role in preserving and enhancing our beloved Fort Kent Outdoor Center for generations to come.
