Memberships

Our memberships follow the calendar year from January 1 to December 31.

Becoming a FKOC member

Adult members will receive a cling-on parking sticker for their vehicle’s windshield that will display their membership. Memberships include access to our beautiful trails year round for Cross Country Skiing, Snowshoeing, Disc Golf, Mountain Biking, Running, and Walking. The Lodge will be open following CDC protocols to members and Security Entrance cards will be issued accordingly. Please take care to keep the Lodge clean and orderly, as it is staffed by volunteers.

Fort Kent Outdoor Center
Membership Options

Membership is the lifeblood of FKOC, providing the primary source of funding that sustains our operations and allows us to offer an array of programs and services. Your membership dollars go directly towards maintaining our trails, grooming ski paths, and ensuring the year-round excellence of our facilities. By becoming a member, you not only gain access to incredible outdoor experiences but also play a pivotal role in preserving and enhancing our beloved Fort Kent Outdoor Center for generations to come.
